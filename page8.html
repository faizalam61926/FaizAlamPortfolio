Broom VR

About

Broom VR is an on-rails shooter game designed for VR platforms. The game follows a captivating story where players must fight dragons and rescue three fairies held captive by the formidable king dragon. Developed using Unreal Engine

Project Info

Role: Programmer, sound, animation
Team Size: 2
Time frame: 2 months
Engine: Unreal Engine
Year: 2021

Introduction:
The Experience was made for Smaash Arcade center, it uses a real broom prop where user mounts on it, it uses telemetry data from unreal to tilt the broom as it does in the games.

On Rail Experience
I made different type of enemy, some follows user, some ambush user, different elemental enemies to give verity, users will follow a pre defined path and shoot enemies along the way.

Live at Locations:
the experience is still live at multiple locations at Smaaash arcade centers.
