Flymax

About

Flymax VR is an innovative VR experience that combines physical and virtual elements to simulate the sensation of flight. Users are mounted on a physical hang glider and equipped with a VR headset, while a large propeller generates wind to enhance the realism.

Project Info

Role: Programmer, sound, Animation
Team Size: 2
Time frame: 1 months
Engine: Unreal Engine
Year: 2021

Introduction:
The Experience was made for Smaaash Arcade center, it uses a real Glider prop where user hangs on, it uses telemetry data from unreal to tilt as it does in the games.

On Rail Experience
Users will follow a pre defined path and let the user experience encounters, i made encounters using Unreal's Sequencer.

Live at Locations:
the experience is still live at multiple locations at Smaaash arcade centers.
