Harry Potter Wand Combat VR

About

A VR Prototype inspired by Hogwarts Legacy, it features wand combat just like portrayed in the movies, the prototype was made as a fan game and a proof of concept.

Project Info

Role: Solo
Team Size: 1
Time frame: 1 week
Engine: Unreal Engine
year: 2022

Introduction:
The prototype was made using Meta Quest 2 headset and Unreal engine, it is a fan game that i made when Hogwarts Legacy was announced. 

Intuitive Controls:
The game supports VR controllers for user input, user can cast spell by selecting them on a spell selection wheel similar to weapon selection wheel in most games, when look at the target and press the trigger button to fire the spell

More to come:
The prototype is a work in progress with many features to come like PVP, small story campaign, more spells, visual overhaul.
