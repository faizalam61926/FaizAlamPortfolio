Blitz Frenzy

About

Local multiplayer Party game where one player wears a VR headset and other join using either phone or computer devices, it is a 1v1 Competitive game, player one becomes survivor who's job is to survive and players two becomes necromancer who's job is to spawn zombies and kill Survivor

Project Info

Role: Programmer
Team Size: 3
Time frame: 5 months
Engine: Unreal Engine
Year: 2022

Introduction:
The game was designed to be a part of a collection, as development continued my team decided to scale up the project and make it a standalone game, so we did. the game is 1v1 local multiplayer party game, user 1 wears a VR headset and user 2 can join using any other devices.

Cross Platform:
the game works on Meta headsets and on Steam VR , it has cross platform compatibility with Android as well so any type of devices can run Blitz Frenzy

Team Management:
we had 3 members working on this project two programmers and one environment artist, I did most of the networked gameplay systems like inventory, shooting, damage and spawning, updating score and completing the game loop, while other programmer worked on networking itself like creating match, Player joining and leaving etc. and environment artist worked on the Map after I designed the level.
